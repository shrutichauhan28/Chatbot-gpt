Company K Leave Request Submission and Approval
1. Purpose
This document outlines the procedures for submitting and approving leave requests at Company K. It aims to ensure that all leave requests are managed efficiently and in accordance with company policies.
2. Scope
This policy applies to all employees seeking to request leave, including annual leave, sick leave, and other types of leave.

3. Leave Request Submission
3.1 Types of Leave
3.1.1 Annual Leave
Description: Paid time off for vacation or personal reasons.
Eligibility: All full-time employees are entitled to annual leave as per their employment contract.
3.1.2 Sick Leave
Description: Paid leave for illness or medical appointments.
Eligibility: All employees are eligible for sick leave as per the company’s sick leave policy.
3.1.3 Other Leave
Description: Includes parental leave, bereavement leave, and unpaid leave.
Eligibility: As per specific company policies and employment contracts.
3.2 Submission Procedure
3.2.1 Online Leave Request
Description: Leave requests should be submitted using the company’s leave management system.
Procedure:
Log In: Access the leave management system using your employee credentials.
Submit Request: Fill out the Leave Request Form with details such as type of leave, start and end dates, and reason for leave.
Attach Documentation: Upload any required documentation (e.g., medical certificates for sick leave).
3.2.2 Manual Submission
Description: For cases where the online system is unavailable, a manual leave request form can be submitted.
Procedure:
Complete Form: Fill out the Manual Leave Request Form.
Submit to HR: Email or hand-deliver the completed form to the HR department.
3.3 Notice Period
Description: Employees should submit leave requests with adequate notice to ensure proper coverage and planning.
Guidelines:
Annual Leave: At least 2 weeks’ notice.
Sick Leave: As soon as possible, ideally before the start of the workday.
Other Leave: As specified in the relevant policy.

4. Leave Request Approval
4.1 Approval Workflow
4.1.1 Initial Review
Description: HR reviews the submitted leave request for completeness and compliance with company policies.
Procedure:
Verification: Ensure all required documentation is attached.
Eligibility Check: Confirm that the leave balance and eligibility criteria are met.
4.1.2 Manager Approval
Description: The request is then forwarded to the employee’s direct manager for approval.
Procedure:
Review: The manager reviews the request in the context of team needs and operational impact.
Decision: Approve or reject the request based on business requirements and leave balance.
4.2 Notification
Description: Employees are notified of the approval or rejection of their leave request.
Procedure:
Notification: Receive an email or system notification regarding the status of the request.
Reason for Rejection: If rejected, the notification will include the reason and any necessary follow-up actions.

5. Leave Management
5.1 Record Keeping
Description: Maintain accurate records of leave requests and approvals.
Procedure:
Documentation: HR will keep records of all approved and rejected leave requests.
Updates: Leave balances and records are updated regularly.
5.2 Leave Balances
Description: Employees can check their leave balances and accruals through the leave management system.
Procedure:
Log In: Access the leave management system to view leave balances.
Inquiries: Contact HR for any discrepancies or questions about leave balances.
5.3 Coverage and Planning
Description: Ensure that work coverage is planned in advance during employee absences.
Procedure:
Team Notification: Inform the team and relevant stakeholders of the employee’s absence.
Work Handover: Coordinate with the employee to ensure a smooth handover of tasks.
